About Me

I currently a postdoctoral researcher in Dr. Ru-Yuan Zhang's Cognitive Computational Neuroscience and Brain Imaging Lab at the Mental Health Center and the School of Psychology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research focuses on building computational models to understand human behavior and brain activity.
